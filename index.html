With Colleague
You:

"Hey, can you believe that guy? Third time this week asking about the yoga class. The schedule's right there on the wall!"

What's happening here?

With members, you use formal, helpful language appropriate for customer service. With colleagues, you switch to informal, conversational language where you can express frustration or share inside perspectives.

(High-pitched, excited voice)
Disciplining Your Pet
You:

"No. Get down. We do not chew on shoes. Bad dog."

What's happening here?

When praising pets, we use a high-pitched, excited tone with simple, repetitive phrases. When disciplining, we switch to a lower, serious tone with clear, direct commands. Pets respond more to tone than specific words.
